The Standard
Residential Clean.

A meticulous top-to-bottom service performed by a two-person team. Every clean follows the same 64-point checklist — kitchen, bathrooms, bedrooms, living areas, and shared spaces — and finishes with a final walk-through.

What's included

  • Dust all surfaces, shelving, and decor
  • Vacuum carpets, rugs, and upholstered furniture
  • Mop hardwood, tile, and laminate floors
  • Disinfect bathrooms — toilet, sink, tub, shower, mirror
  • Clean kitchen — counters, exterior of appliances, stovetop, sink
  • Empty all wastebaskets and replace liners
  • Make beds and tidy soft furnishings
  • Wipe down doorknobs, switches, and high-touch surfaces
  • Streak-free clean on interior glass and mirrors
  • Final walk-through and lemon-eucalyptus mist
