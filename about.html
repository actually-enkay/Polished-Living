About

Founded on a
quiet obsession.

Polished Living started in 2019 with a single team of three friends who couldn't find a cleaning service that took the work as seriously as we wanted to.

Six years later, we're a team of 28 across Chicago and the North Shore. We've never franchised, never used subcontractors, and never raised our prices without telling our customers first. Every cleaner is W-2, paid above market, and trained in a four-week apprenticeship before they're allowed in a client's home alone.

We believe a clean home is a quiet kind of luxury. Not flashy. Not posted on Instagram. Just yours, every time you walk in the door.
