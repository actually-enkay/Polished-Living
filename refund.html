This Refund Policy explains when and how Polished Living ("we," "us," "our") issues refunds for our cleaning services. It works alongside our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

1. Our satisfaction guarantee

We stand behind every clean. If you are not satisfied with any aspect of our work, please contact us within 24 hours of service completion. We will send a team back at no additional charge to address any issue covered by your original service. This is our "redo guarantee" and is our primary remedy for service complaints. We believe a redo is almost always better than a refund — the goal is to leave you with a home you love, not money back.

2. When we issue refunds

We will issue a full or partial refund in the following circumstances:

  • Service not performed: If our team did not arrive or did not complete the booked service, and no rescheduling has been arranged, you will receive a full refund.
  • Redo not feasible: If we cannot return to redo a service within 7 days of your complaint, and the issue is clearly within the scope of our original work, we will issue a partial refund based on the unfinished portion.
  • Damage to property: Damage claims are handled under our insurance and the "Damage and liability" section of our Terms of Service rather than under this Refund Policy.
  • Billing errors: Charges made in error (duplicate charges, incorrect amounts) will be refunded in full upon verification.

3. When we do not issue refunds

We do not issue refunds in the following circumstances:

  • Complaints made more than 24 hours after service completion
  • Issues caused by use of the property after our team has left (e.g. cooking, dirt tracked in, pets)
  • Services that were performed to the published standard, even if the result does not match your subjective expectations
  • Add-ons or specialty services that were performed but you decide afterward you did not need
  • Pre-existing damage, wear, stains, or conditions disclosed or visible at the time of service
  • Bookings cancelled within our cancellation window (see Cancellation, below)

4. Cancellation fees and refunds

Cancellation timing affects whether you receive a refund:

  • More than 48 hours before service: Free cancellation; full refund of any payment collected.
  • Within 48 hours but more than 24 hours before service: 50% cancellation fee; we refund the remaining 50%.
  • Within 24 hours of service, or no-show: 100% cancellation fee; no refund.
  • Cancellations by Polished Living: If we cancel due to weather, illness, or other circumstances on our end, you receive a full refund or your preferred reschedule.

5. How to request a refund

To request a refund, email hello@polishedliving.com within the applicable time window with:

  • Your name and the date of service
  • A description of the issue
  • Photos, where relevant
  • What outcome you are requesting (redo, refund, or partial refund)

We will respond within two business days. Approved refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days, returned to the original payment method.

6. Chargebacks

If you believe a charge is incorrect, please contact us first so we can resolve the issue directly. Initiating a chargeback before allowing us to investigate may result in suspension of future service and additional administrative fees.

7. Recurring service customers

For customers on recurring service plans, refunds apply per individual visit and do not affect the recurring arrangement. You may cancel a recurring plan at any time with no cancellation fee, provided that any individual visit cancellations follow the standard window above.

8. Disputes

If we cannot agree on a refund outcome, the dispute will be handled under the dispute resolution provisions of our Terms of Service.
